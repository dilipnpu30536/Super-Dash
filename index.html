    INTRODUCTION

    AIMS is a system of automating payroll processing and financial operations. The system is comprehensive and user-friendly in carrying out the financial activities in the railway office. IPAS is mainly divided into two categories.

    The accounting reform will position Indian Railways to efficiently prepare financial statements that comply with accounting standards and reflect the profits/losses in providing services, manufacturing and maintenance across Indian Railways and according to lines of business and service through the definition, design, development and implementation of a robust, modern financial management information system that is commensurate with and aligned to the business imperatives of Indian Railways. It has two phases: a design phase and an implementation phase.
